Welcome to the CairoCoin API

Conveniently track everything related to the parallel market in Egypt. This is the initial version, and we are actively adding more indicators for a more precise market monitoring experience. Through this API, you can gain detailed insights into the black market exchange rate for the dollar, Binance rates, arbitrage dollar prices, as well as gold and bullion prices. Additionally, you can access Egypt's credit rating. Stay tuned for further enhancements and updates!
